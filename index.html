You're Not Imagining It

Your child isn't being defiant. Your parenting isn't failing. And you're not alone in feeling completely exhausted.

Pathological Demand Avoidance (PDA) is a neurotype within autism where everyday requests trigger intense anxiety. What works for other autistic children - structure, rewards, clear expectations - can actually make things worse for PDA kids.

You need support designed specifically for PDA. Not generic autism advice. Not behavioral strategies. Real help for real moments.
